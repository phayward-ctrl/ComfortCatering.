Advance Notice

The amount of notice required depends on the size of the event and the volume of food being ordered. Customers should generally place orders between 48 and 120 hours in advance.

Larger functions and special requests may require additional preparation time. Fresh local fish should be requested at least seven days in advance.

Customized Service

We understand that every event and customer is different. Comfort Catering provides a service to its customers, so we work with them to create food selections that fit the occasion, preferences, guest count, and budget.
